\section{Objectives}
The objectives of this lecture are to:
\begin{itemize}
\item Illustrate the use of Robin boundary conditions with another example.
\item Show how to solve BVPs with an unknown parameter with \lstinline[style=myMatlab]{bvp5c}.
\end{itemize}
